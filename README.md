# Insurance-data-analysis

The dataset used in this project contains historical insurance records designed for predictive modeling of claim likelihood. It includes a comprehensive set of features describing policyholders, their behavior, and contextual risk factors.

**Key Features**

**Policyholder Information**:
Demographic attributes such as age, gender, occupation, marital status, income level, and geographic location.

**Claim History**:
Historical claim data including claim frequency, claim type (e.g., medical, automotive, property), claim amounts, and duration between claims.

**Policy Details**:
Information related to insurance policies such as coverage type, policy duration, premium amount, deductibles, and policy tenure.

**Risk Indicators**:
Features representing risk exposure, including credit score, driving history (for auto insurance), health indicators (for life/medical insurance), and property-related risk attributes.

**External Factors**:
Environmental and macroeconomic variables such as weather conditions, inflation trends, and regulatory changes that may influence claim behavior.
